Oleo®THC Powder

Oleo THC powder is revolutionizing the way THC is consumed.
Healthier way consume Cannabis.

Orally consumed cannabis is inherently safer for you than any form of smoking is. Orally consumed cannabis can be unappealing to some people because the effects take longer to feel, there is more uncertainty in dosing, and eating THC just feels different than smoking it does. Here at Oleo we’ve aimed to solve those problems, and believe we have.

Higher Bio-Availability

Bioavailability means how effectively a substance can be absorbed into the body to have an effect. THC has a 6-20% oral bioavailability in part because it is ‘fat soluble’. Our products have greatly increased bioavailability because the THC is encased in carbohydrates. Because our products dissolve in water the THC is easier for your body to absorb; which lets you feel the effects quicker.

A Truly Water-Soluable THC Powder

THC does not dissolve into water without the use of questionable additives like Propylene Glycol. Our products have no questionable additives. We only use safe and natural carbohydrates to entrap individual THC molecules thereby making the THC water soluble.
